Pancit Recipe

Description:

Pancit is to the Filipino as lo mein is to the Chinese and Pad Thai is to the Thai. It is the quintessential noodle dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Carrots (diced)
  • 1 Medium yellow onion (diced)
  • 2 Cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 Packet dry vermicelli noodles
  • Soy sauce
  • MSG
